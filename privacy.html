Privacy Policy for Schultility

- Benjamin Gerresheim, 06.07.2025

I value your privacy. This privacy policy explains that my Chrome Extension, Schultility, does not collect any personal data from its users.

Data Collection and Usage

Schultility is designed to run entirely on the user's computer. It does not collect, store, share, or transmit any personal information, browsing history, or any other user-specific data. All operations and functionalities are performed locally within your browser.

No Tracking

I do not use any tracking cookies, analytics services, or third-party scripts to monitor your activity.

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy or the extension, please feel free to contact me via E-mail.
